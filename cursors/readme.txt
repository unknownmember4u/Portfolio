Step 2: '''Then, open up Mouse settings on your computer/laptop.'''

Step 3: '''Next, Press on (for windows 10) Additional mouse settings'''

Step 4: '''Now replace/double click all the cursors with the cursors you downloaded. (example: replace Normal with Normal.cur) '''

There, you are now done!
